Aircraft Spare Parts

Sourcing & supply of certified, airworthy components for commercial, regional, helicopter and business-jet fleets worldwide.

Components & categories we handle:

  • Aircraft engines & APU units (serviceable / overhauled)
  • Avionics systems – FMS, transponders, radios, displays
  • Landing gear assemblies & components
  • Airframe structural parts & panels
  • Hydraulic & pneumatic system components
  • Interior parts – seats, galleys, overhead panels
  • Rotary-wing components (rotor blades, gearboxes)
  • Consumables & expendables (filters, seals, O-rings)

Aircraft Types Supported:

Boeing 737 / 777 / 787 Airbus A320 / A330 / A350 Bombardier / Embraer Gulfstream / Dassault Falcon Bell / Airbus H135 / H145 Sikorsky / Leonardo AW
📋

Quality & Compliance Standards

Every part we source is fully traceable with complete documentation, meeting international airworthiness requirements.

Documentation & certifications provided:

  • EASA Form 1 / FAA 8130-3 release certificates
  • Serviceable tags with full traceability records
  • Airworthiness Directives (AD) compliance records
  • Component Maintenance Manuals (CMM) references
  • Aircraft-on-Ground (AOG) priority handling available
  • Lot certification for consumables & expendables
  • Vendor approvals from certified MRO facilities
  • Export documentation & customs clearance support

AOG Support: We understand the critical nature of Aircraft-on-Ground situations. Our network allows priority sourcing with rapid worldwide logistics to minimise downtime.

Pre-Owned Aircraft

Second-Hand Helicopters & Private Jets

We connect qualified buyers with verified sellers of pre-owned rotary-wing and fixed-wing aircraft — providing a discreet, professional brokerage service backed by thorough due diligence and legal compliance.

🚁

Pre-Owned Helicopters

Light single-engine to heavy twin-engine rotorcraft for executive, offshore, EMS, and utility operations.

Helicopter categories available:

  • Light turbine – Robinson R66, Airbus H225/H125
  • Medium twin – Airbus H135, H145, Bell 429
  • Super-medium – Airbus H155, Leonardo AW139
  • Heavy twin – Sikorsky S-92, Leonardo AW101
  • Corporate & VIP configured rotorcraft
  • Medevac / EMS equipped helicopters
  • Offshore oil & gas support helicopters

Typical buyers: Private individuals, charter operators, air ambulance services, oil & gas companies, government & military clients.

🛩️

Pre-Owned Private Jets

Very Light Jets to ultra-long-range business jets — sourced globally and presented with complete technical & financial transparency.

Private jet categories available:

  • Very Light Jets (VLJ) – Embraer Phenom 100, Eclipse 550
  • Light jets – Cessna Citation, Pilatus PC-24
  • Midsize jets – Bombardier Learjet 75, Hawker 900
  • Super-midsize – Cessna Citation Latitude, Gulfstream G280
  • Large cabin – Bombardier Challenger 604/605/650
  • Ultra-long-range – Gulfstream G650ER, Global 7500
  • VIP-configured airliners (ACJ / BBJ)

Typical buyers: HNWIs, family offices, corporate flight departments, charter operators, fractional ownership programs.
